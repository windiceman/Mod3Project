Got IT?

Our Mission

Welcome to Got IT?! This website is an aggregate of information displaying the skills needed for aspiring young professionals, as well as established professionals looking to brush up on their knowledge, to be successful.

We will be highlighting various technical and professional skills that are imperative to comprehend prior to entering into the corporate world. We will also shine light on basic fundamentals that often get overlooked. These fundamentals are far too often disregarded, but can have a tremendous impact on our careers! We will have course information dedicated to computer hardware, customer service, interviews, and grammar.

In the hardware course we will discuss the proper installation of various hardware components. This includes how to properly install a hard drive, upgrade ram, expansion slots, amongst other things. We will have a video to demonstrate the hands-on activity so that users can have a visual representation of the information we are presenting to them. In the customer service course we will explain how to provide good customer service and the importance of customer satisfaction. We will show various examples of what good customer service looks like and share tips on how to improve customer service. In the interview course we will have a list of do’s and don’ts of interviewing as well as a song to share some tips to follow when conducting an interview so that the information is easier to retain. In the grammar course will highlight small grammatical errors that many professionals overlook as well a video to demonstrate the information.
